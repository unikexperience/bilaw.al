Bilaw.al Hameed

At best, I am a passionate builder. I build software, companies, communities, and people. I aim to design world-class products that ooze excellence and perfection by imperfection. I work hard to listen and to innovate every day.

During my career to date, I have built three startups (one w/ seed investment), built circa. 50 projects, received numerous mentions, attended 3 events & 9 hackathons (and won 3) and a "Top Developer" award by Dev8D.

I've built a reverse image search startup, social quiz app, mobile chatroulette, bus timetable, TFGM Ruby gem, Cheddar PHP API, WordPress plugin, fundraiser website, viral website and more. As impressive as it is, I haven't made any real, life-changing impact from my work. Until I do, it has minimal value for me.

I love startups. I'm glad that it's now the social norm to be an entrepreneur, with minimal investment and risk. I try my best to make mistakes, learn from them, and tell stories to people. I want to spread the freedom of running a startup. I know that, one day in the future, I will start another company.

In my day-to-day, I am studying for a Computer Science degree. I spend the majority of my free time exploring the world. I would normally recite a plentiful of clichés concerning the meaning of life, but I figured you're too cool for that. On that note, I also try to make it easier for people to learn.

I constantly maintain my health through gym, football and meditation. I'm an avid Manchester United but an even bigger FC Barcelona fan, and I play 5-a-side football regularly. I'm also exploring meditation as a way to think smarter, not harder.
